About me

Hey, my name is Kevin! I quite like systems programming and computer graphics – my languages of choice are Rust and C++ (sometimes). Currently, I am quite obsessed with emulation development and its exponentially growing complexity.

Having only written a simple CHIP-8 emulator so far, the next step is of course a Gameboy emulator (DMG) which is currently in progress! From time to time, I also dabble in computer graphics as that is my main point of study at university – from raytracers in CUDA to simple mesh processing.
