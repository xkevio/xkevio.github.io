About me

Hey, my name is Kevin! I quite enjoy systems programming, computer graphics and typesetting – my languages of choice are Rust, C++ and Typst. Currently, I am quite obsessed with emulation development and its exponentially growing complexity.

I’ve developed emulators for the following systems:

  • CHIP-8 – the “Hello, World” of emulation development.
  • Color Game Boy – try it right here!
  • Game Boy Advance – featuring an experimental JIT with cranelift.

I plan to tackle either the Nintendo DS or PlayStation 1 next to emulate a system with 3D graphics. From time to time, I also dabble in computer graphics as that is my main point of study at university; or typesetting with Typst to ensure beautifully formatted papers.
